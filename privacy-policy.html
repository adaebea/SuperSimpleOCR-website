Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how SuperSimpleOCR (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the SuperSimpleOCR application and its associated support website. By using our services, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

SuperSimpleOCR runs locally on your Mac. The app does not transmit your screenshots or recognized text to our servers. We may collect limited information when you contact us for support:

  • Contact details you provide voluntarily (such as your email).
  • Diagnostic information you choose to share (screenshots, logs, or descriptions of an issue).

How We Use Information

We use the information you send us solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance.
  • Improve the stability and usability of SuperSimpleOCR.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We only share information when required by law or if necessary to investigate and prevent abuse of our services.

Data Retention

Emails and diagnostic files you send us are retained only while we work on your request. Once resolved, we delete the correspondence unless we need to keep it for legal or auditing purposes.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect any information you share with us. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may request that we:

  • Provide a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate details.
  • Delete correspondence we store, unless retention is legally required.

To exercise these rights, contact us at adaebea@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Significant updates may also be announced via the support site.
